Taste: The taste of a food should be accurately represented by having the 5 main tastes all with 256 options.

Component Type Number of Bits Description
Saltyness Number 1 Byte (0-255) The amount of salt in the food should be represented well with 256 choices.
Sweetness Number 1 Byte (0-255) The sweetness level of a food should be accurately represented with 256 choices.
Spicyness Number 1 Byte (0-255) The spicyness level of a food should be accurately represented with 256 choices.
Bitterness Number 1 Byte (0-255) The bitterness level of a food should be accurately represented with 256 choices.
Sourness Number 1 Byte (0-255) The sourness level of a food should be accurately represented with 256 choices.
