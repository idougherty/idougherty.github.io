Classroom

The purpose of this classroom is to have the students sit on the side with the blue carpet for lectures or when the teacher is giving instructions, then they sit in the three groups to work on projects. At the blue carpet, no one sits infront of another person, this encourages more communication and involvement. At the three groups, there would be computers of the students to work on, the computers' monitors will be tracked and able to be veiwed by the teacher at any time from his computer.
